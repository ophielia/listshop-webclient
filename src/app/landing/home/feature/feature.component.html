features of the list shop

  • targeted shopping

    Shop for the meals you'll make. Less stress. Less waste.

  • Share Lists

    Everyone in the household can add to the "fridge list".

  • Add Dishes to your List

    Enter the ingredients you need for a dish. Then, add that dish to a shopping list with the touch of a button.

  • flexibility

    Copy or move items from one list to another. Combine lists.

  • Personalized

    keeps the items which you add and remove from lists handy.

  • Get a jump start

    Create a starter list of the things you get - or want to remind yourself to get) each big shopping trip.
